# Acknowledgments

Ferrink is independently written Rust and Slint code. These projects helped us
understand the landscape or sharpen the interface; their code and assets were
not copied unless a separate notice says otherwise.

## Direct dependencies and staged assets

- [Slint](https://github.com/slint-ui/slint) — Ferrink's UI toolkit.
- [Born2Root/Fast-Font](https://github.com/Born2Root/Fast-Font) — optional
  reading-font workflow; its included notices are retained.

## Hardware, lifecycle, and reader research

- [KOReader](https://github.com/koreader/koreader) — reader-app behavior and
  launcher handoff research.
- [KindleModding/sh_integration](https://github.com/KindleModding/sh_integration)
  — lifecycle research only; no repository-wide license was identified, so no
  implementation was copied.
- [terpinedream/OpenReader](https://github.com/terpinedream/OpenReader) and
  [usetrmnl/trmnl-kindle](https://github.com/usetrmnl/trmnl-kindle) — E Ink
  integration research.
- [FBInk](https://github.com/NiLuJe/FBInk) — framebuffer and refresh vocabulary.

## UI and product inspiration

- [floers/ordinary](https://gitlab.com/floers/ordinary),
  [vivi-ui/vivi](https://codeberg.org/vivi-ui/vivi), and
  [mayocream/koharu](https://github.com/mayocream/koharu) — compact UI ideas.
- [uxjulia/crossink](https://github.com/uxjulia/crossink) and
  [crosspoint-reader/crosspoint-reader](https://github.com/crosspoint-reader/crosspoint-reader)
  — small-screen reader ergonomics.
- [kapoorankush/litclock](https://github.com/kapoorankush/litclock) — the
  general idea of a time-aware literary display. Ferrink does not include its
  code or any third-party excerpt corpus.

Thank you to the maintainers and communities behind these projects. If an
acknowledgment is incomplete, please open an issue with the relevant license or
source information.
